// TL;DR — The Short Version

This is a static student advocacy website. We do not collect any personally identifiable information. We use Google Analytics (GA4) with IP anonymization enabled to count anonymous visitor traffic — we can see how many people visited and from which country, but never who you are. No accounts, no forms, no personal data stored by us.

01 Overview

The E-Cycle: Every Spark Matters campaign website ("the Site") is a student-run advocacy project created by Class Section CPE12 of the Computer Engineering program at De La Salle University Dasmariñas. This Privacy Policy explains what information, if any, is collected when you use the Site and how it is handled.

By accessing the Site, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this policy.

02 Data We Collect

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to collect anonymous, aggregated data about how visitors interact with this Site. This helps us understand the reach of the E-Cycle campaign. IP anonymization is enabled, meaning your full IP address is never stored. The data collected includes:

📊 Anonymous page views & visit counts 🌍 Approximate country / city (not exact) 📱 Device type, browser & OS 🔗 Referral source (how you found the site)

The following are never collected by us:

✗ No names or email addresses ✗ No full IP addresses (anonymized) ✗ No login or account data ✗ No form submissions stored ✗ No payment or sensitive data
03 Third-Party Services

Certain features of the Site load resources from third-party providers. These services operate under their own privacy policies, which we have no control over.

Google Analytics (GA4) — We use GA4 to anonymously track visitor counts and behavior. Google may set cookies (such as _ga) on your device to distinguish visitors. IP anonymization is enabled. You can opt out via the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on. See Google's Privacy Policy.

Google Fonts — Typography on this Site is served via Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com). Google may log your IP address as part of this request. See Google's Privacy Policy.

Google Maps Embed — The Drop-Off Locator page embeds Google Maps using the free iframe embed. When you view a map, Google may set its own cookies or log your IP address. See Google's Privacy Policy.

Facebook — Links to our Facebook page are provided for your convenience. Clicking these links takes you to Facebook, which is governed by Meta's Privacy Policy. We do not embed Facebook widgets or pixels on this Site.

04 External Links

The Site contains links to external websites (news articles, academic references, government pages, Google Maps). These sites are not operated by us. Once you leave this Site, we encourage you to review the privacy policy of any external site you visit. We have no responsibility or liability for their content or practices.

05 Children's Privacy

This Site does not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from any visitors, including children. The anonymous analytics data collected via GA4 contains no personal identifiers. The campaign content is informational and suitable for general audiences.

06 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the Site evolves. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Since we only collect anonymous, aggregated analytics data, there is no personally identifiable information to delete or request. If you have concerns about data collected by Google Analytics, you may use the Google Analytics Opt-out Add-on.

Questions about this policy? Reach out to any member of the CPE12 team — we're happy to clarify anything.
